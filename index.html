    17th Dec 13:00 openEHR workshop Session 3

    Agenda

    This final workshop will be much more interactive and underpinned by focussing on the information requirements a proposed 'ReSPECT+' application to handle the patient CPR decision, along with other anticipatory care information and end of life planning, drawing initially from the My Care Choices dataset and other UK examples.

    The aim here is to get a better understanding of the information flows and needs of the community, then make a start at defining these, using the openEHR archetypes and templates.

    We will not be designing the application itself or delving into the technical aspects, though we may be able to build a simple demo app towards the end of the session.
